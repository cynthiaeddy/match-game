how to play

  • Click on a card to show a car.
  • Click on a second card to show another car.

  • If the cars match, the cards stay revealed.
  • If there is no match, the cards are hidden and you can click another pair.

  • You can click the little car below at any time to restart or play a new game.
  • When you have correctly matched all 6 pairs of cards, the game's completed.
